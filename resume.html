Personal Details

Since high school, I have always been fascinated with computers and things that I can create using computers. This led me to focus my studies on computer studies at degree level and pursued a career as a computer tutor.

On completion, I engage in content creation as a freelancer working for different online platforms such as Fiverr, academic writers etc. This experience was engaging as I got to work with different clients all over the world. I also got a chance to sharpen my skills as a front-end developer.

Ultimately, I decided to transition to tutoring and got hired as a secondary school teacher in Kenya, Kirinyaga county. Working with students has been one of the most fulfilling career paths that I have chosen. This has also given me a time to develop my interpersonal skills through teamwork with the administration and colleagues.
